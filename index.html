TriNova Inc

Exhibitor: Liquid Properties - pH and Density

Participants will learn about pH and density using various liquids (food safe), scales, pH indicators (red cabbage) and pH Probes/transmitters. Have equal amounts of sample fluids in mason jars which kids can weigh on mass scales to guess density. They will then mix the liquids and see how the differences in densities will cause the liquids to separate. Participants will then use a pH indicator solution as a pH indicator to guess the "order" of pH of the liquid using a color chart. These answers will then be verified using an Endress + Hauser pH probe by dipping it into the liquid.
